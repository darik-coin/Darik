* Preparation for the next major ecosystem announcement.

**Target Timeline:** Following Liquidity Milestone

## Phase 3 — Treasury Migration

**Target Timeline:** Within 1 Month

* Planned migration of approximately **17,850,000 DARIK** from secure cold-storage infrastructure to an operational treasury wallet.

* Enhanced transparency regarding treasury management.

## Phase 4 — Strategic Investor Participation

**Target Timeline:** Within 4 Months

* Planned allocation of treasury-held DARIK to selected strategic and institutional investors.
* Focus on long-term ecosystem growth, liquidity strengthening, and network development.
* Expansion of the DARIK investor base through large-scale strategic partnerships.

## Phase 5 — Real-World Commodity Settlement

**Target Timeline:** Within 6 Months

* Development of a commodity settlement framework utilizing DARIK as a medium of exchange.
* Targeting large-volume commodity transactions with destination-based delivery mechanisms.
* Integration of DARIK into real-world trade and settlement processes.
* Advancement toward practical commercial utility within global markets.

---

## Long-Term Vision

DARIK aims to bridge blockchain technology, real-world commerce, and transparent value transfer through a growing ecosystem focused on utility, liquidity, and sustainable adoption.

*This roadmap reflects current development objectives and may be adjusted based on market conditions, technical requirements, regulatory considerations, and ecosystem priorities.*
